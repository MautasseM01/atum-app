# Paradise — The Walled Garden

**Arabic root:** فردوس (firdaws = enclosed garden, from Old Persian *pairidaeza*)

**Confidence:** 🔬

---

## The Original Meaning

Paradise comes from Old Persian *pairi* (around) + *daeza* (wall) = the garden enclosed on all sides.
It passed into Arabic as *firdaws*, then into Greek *paradeisos*, then English *paradise*.

This wasn't just "a beautiful place" — it was a **specific architectural structure**: complete enclosure, protected interior, a boundary that makes the inside sacred.

---

## The Physics Connection

TOR is the root of rotation, structure, and enclosure — the energy of the Torus.
A torus (donut shape) is exactly this: a circle wrapping around itself from the outside, protecting what's inside.
Paradise = the perfect torus: complete enclosure, inner balance, self-sustaining structure.
