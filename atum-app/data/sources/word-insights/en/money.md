# money

**Arabic Root:** مَالَ (M-W-L) — to own, to possess, to incline toward
**Cosmic Root:** ATUM — accumulated potential, energy held in reserve
**Confidence:** 🧩

---

## The Deep Meaning

*Money* from Latin *moneta* = the epithet of Juno Moneta, Roman goddess of the mint.
Arabic *māl* = property, wealth, that toward which you incline = ATUM as retention.
Currency = energy frozen into a portable, exchangeable form.

---

## The Physics

Money = frozen energy (ATUM): it stores the value of labor and time.
The flow of money through an economy = BULL.
Its distribution through a system = TOR.
Concentrated wealth = static ATUM — a healthy economy requires the cycle.

---

## In Your Daily Life

Money creates no value while stationary — it creates value when it flows.
The core of any economic system: convert ATUM (savings) → BULL (investment) → TOR (cycle).

---

## Why This Matters

Arabic *māl* (wealth), *mayl* (inclination), *mīl* (mile, as in the measured distance) — all from the same root: the gravitational pull toward something.
Money is what you "incline toward" — social gravity. 🧩
