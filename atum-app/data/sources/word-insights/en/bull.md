# Bull — The Symbol of Outward Radiation

**Arabic root:** ب-ل (balla = to spread moisture/energy outward, to reach, to convey)
**Cosmic root:** BULL 🔴
**Confidence:** 🧩 (Bonacci, EB_series)

---

## The Original Meaning

Bull = the ox. But why an ox?
In ancient cultures, the bull symbolized **explosive outward energy**: strength, fertility, vital radiation.
The B-L root in Arabic describes spreading: *balla* (spread moisture), *ballagha* (convey/send), *balagha* (reach the destination).

---

## The Physics Connection

BULL = outward radiating energy. Light radiates, sound expands, the sun emits.

## Why It Matters

The bull in Phoenician script became the first letter: **Aleph** (from "alf" = bull).
The first letter = symbol of the first energy = outward radiation.
Every alphabet begins with the bull. 🧩
