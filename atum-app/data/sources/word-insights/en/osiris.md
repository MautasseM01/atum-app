# osiris

**Arabic Root:** أُسّ / أَزَرَ (A-S-S / A-Z-R) — the foundation, that which supports from within
**Cosmic Root:** TOR — the cycle of death and rebirth, life as a circle
**Confidence:** 🔍🧩

---

## The Deep Meaning

*Osiris* from ancient Egyptian *Wsir* = the throne, the power, "the very mighty one."
Osiris = god of resurrection and fertility — died, was dismembered, then reassembled = ultimate TOR.
Semitic root *'zr* = *'azara* / *'azada* = to support, to reinforce = the power of inner support.

---

## The Physics

The Osiris myth = cosmic TOR: death (ATUM) → dissolution (BULL) → resurrection (TOR).
The Nile itself embodies this: it dries (death) → floods (rebirth) → fertilizes (life).
Life is not a line but a circle — this is TOR.

## In Your Daily Life

Every seasonal cycle = Osiris: the autumn tree ≠ death, it is ATUM waiting for TOR.
Sleep itself = a small death preceding a daily resurrection.
