# Baal — The Radiant Lord

**Arabic root:** ب-ع-ل (ba'ala = to master, to be lord; ba'l = husband = master of the house)
**Cosmic root:** BULL 🔴
**Confidence:** 🔬 (Dawood, Al-Qubaysi)

---

## The Original Meaning

*Baal* = lord, master, owner — the entity that radiates power and authority.
Baal was the storm and fertility god in Semitic civilizations — explosive vital energy.
Spread through Phoenician into: Bal, Bel, Baal across dozens of languages.

---

## The Physics Connection

BULL = energy radiating outward with force, explosion of vitality.
The lord = the source of cosmic energy. Baal = the great radiating power = BULL at its highest expression.

## In Everyday Life

- **Baal** = ancient Semitic god name
- **Ball** = B-L root = the shape of balanced radiation
- **Balance** = equilibrium between two B-L forces
- Place names: **Baalbek** (house of Baal), Baal-Hermon

## Why It Matters

English "ball," Canaanite god "Baal," and Arabic "balla" (to spread) descend from the same phonetic root.
The root manifests equally in everyday words and sacred names.
