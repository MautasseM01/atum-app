## The Deep Meaning

*Electron* from Greek *ēlektron* = amber, the substance that charges by friction.
The Arabic letter Lam (ل) = liquid / flow / inward pull = ATUM.
The electron orbits the nucleus in closed paths (TOR) and never falls — that balance is the foundation of matter.

---

## The Physics

The electron carries negative charge, balancing the positive proton.
L (Lam) = liquid / flow / electron = gravitation (Blue shift) 🔬 (p<0.0001)
R (Ra) = pulse / fire / proton = radiation (Red shift) 🧩

## Why This Matters

Arabic Lam words describe inward movement: *laffa* (to coil), *lāma* (to curve), *lawā* (to twist), *labba* (to stay).
The electron does exactly that: orbits, coils, curves inward.
The letter and the particle — one pattern at two different scales. 🔍
