## The Original Meaning

Jordan ← Aramaic/Hebrew *Yarden* = **the descending river**, the one that flows downward.
The Jordan River is the lowest river on Earth (empties into the Dead Sea at −430m).
Arabic *warada* = to descend to water. *Mawrid* = water source. *Warda* (rose) = plant that seeks water.

---

## The Physics Connection

TOR = cyclic descent and rotation. Water naturally flows from high to low = the physical manifestation of gravity's cycle.
The Jordan preserved its name for thousands of years — geography is captured and preserved by language.
