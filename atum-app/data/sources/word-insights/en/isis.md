## The Original Meaning

Isis ← Arabic *hayyizi* = "she of the hayyiz" = **the one who encloses all existence**.
*Al-hayyiz* in Arabic: the space a body occupies, the field surrounding something.
In ancient Egyptian mythology, Isis is the cosmic mother who encloses and protects all things.

Sound shift: ح (h) → silent (regular Semitic phonetic rule — guttural to vowel onset) 🔬
