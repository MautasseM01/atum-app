# abyss

**Arabic Root:** هَوَى (H-W-Y) — to fall, to love, the empty space
**Cosmic Root:** ATUM — before the beginning, the first void
**Confidence:** 🔬🧩

---

## The Deep Meaning

*Abyss* from Greek *abyssos* = without bottom (a + byssos).
Arabic *hawā* = falling into emptiness, and from it *hawā'* (air/space).
Semitic *tehom* = the abyss in Genesis — "darkness on the face of the deep."
ATUM = what precedes everything: silence, void, absolute possibility.

---

## The Physics

The abyss = absolute ATUM state: infinite density, or absolute vacuum.
A black hole = contracted ATUM: gravity so strong nothing escapes.
Before the Big Bang = an informational abyss = pure ATUM.

---

## In Your Daily Life

The abyss is not annihilation — it is pure potential.
"In the beginning was the void" — the abyss precedes all creation.

---

## Why This Matters

*Hawā* (falling) / *hawā* (love) / *hawā'* (air) — three meanings from one root.
Falling, loving, and emptiness: the root sees them as the same — attraction toward the formless. 🔬🧩
