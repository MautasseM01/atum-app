## The Deep Meaning

*Hero* from Greek *hērōs* = warrior, protector, demigod.
Arabic *ḥarra* = heat, inner fire = BULL in its human form.
A hero is someone whose energy overflows outward, affecting those around them.

---

## The Physics

BULL = radiating energy that pushes outward.
The hero radiates energy: courage, protection, inspiration — all outward directions.
Its opposite: fear = ATUM contracted, a withdrawal inward.
