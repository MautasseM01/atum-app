# Tour / Tur — The Spiral of Time and Space

**Arabic root:** ط-و-ر (tawwara = to rotate, to evolve, to pass from one stage to the next)
**Cosmic root:** TOR 🔵
**Confidence:** 🔬 (Dawood, Al-Qubaysi)

---

## The Original Meaning

Arabic *tawr*: a stage, a cycle, a transformation from one state to the next.
*Tatawwur* (evolution) = passing through *atwar* (stages) = rotating in ascending circles.
TOR cosmic energy = rotation and cyclic structure — the Torus.

English "tour" = a circular journey that returns to the starting point. 🔬

---

## The Physics Connection

- Galaxies follow toroidal paths
- Tornados = air torus in motion
- DNA = a toroidal spiral
- Electromagnetic energy = rotating waves

TOR root words describe anything that turns, cycles, or structures itself.
