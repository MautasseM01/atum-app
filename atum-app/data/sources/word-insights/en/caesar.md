## The Original Meaning

One golden chain, all from a single root: **sara** (to lead, to walk):

**سار (Sara)** → **Caesar** → **Kaiser** (German) → **Tsar** (Russian) → **Sire** (French) → **Sir** (English)

6 languages, 3 continents, 3,000 years — the same root. 🔬
