# Atlas — The Stable Bearer

**Arabic root:** ع-ت-ل / ط-ل-س (atala = to carry heavily and steadily)
**Cosmic root:** ATUM 🟢
**Confidence:** 🔍

---

## The Original Meaning

Atlas in Greek mythology = the giant who holds up the vault of heaven on his shoulders.
In Arabic: *al-atil* = one who carries with force and stability. *Atalla* = to stand firm and overlook.
The Atlas Mountains in Morocco = the mountains bearing the sky (highest point in North Africa).

---

## The Physics Connection

ATUM = stability, inertia, and bearing. Newton's laws:
- A body resists change = Atlas resists the weight of the sky
- The mountain stays fixed = ATUM at its highest physical expression

---

## In Everyday Life

- **Atlas** (book of maps) = the book that "carries" the whole world between its covers
- **Anatomy atlas** = the book carrying the body's map
- **Atlas vertebra** (C1) = the vertebra carrying the weight of the skull
- **Atlantic Ocean** = the ocean bordered by the Atlas mountains

---

## Why It Matters

Anything that carries, stabilizes, and resists = ATUM energy. Atlas in every form is the root of stability.
