# republic

**Arabic Root:** جمهور (J-M-H-R) — the large group, the crowd, the collective
**Cosmic Root:** TOR — the cycle, rotation, power returning to the people
**Confidence:** 🧩

---

## The Deep Meaning

*Republic* from Latin *res publica* = the public thing, that which belongs to all.
Arabic *jumhūr* = the large mass of people.
TOR = that which turns and returns — power that returns to the people = political TOR.

---

## The Physics

A TOR political system: power is given by the people → exercised → returned to the people via election.
When this rotation stops = static ATUM (concentrated power) = authoritarianism.

---

## In Your Daily Life

Every election cycle = TOR restoring balance.
*Jumhūr* is the Arabic word chosen to translate *res publica* — they chose the right root. 🧩

---

## Why This Matters

Public / jumhūr / pueblo / peuple — the Proto-Indo-European root *\*peuk-* = the full, the dense, the crowd.
In every language, the concept of "republic" describes rotation: power passes through everyone.
