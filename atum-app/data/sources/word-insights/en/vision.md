## The Original Meaning

"Vision" from Latin *videre* = to see. Proto-Indo-European root *weid-* = to see, to know.
In Arabic: *ra'a* (to see) and *mir'a* (mirror = that which radiates the image outward).
Sight = light radiates from the object and reaches the eye — BULL energy.
