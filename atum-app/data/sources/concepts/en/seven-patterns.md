# The Seven Patterns and Sacred Language

---

## What Is a Pattern?

A "pattern" in language = the law that generates infinite words from one root.
In Arabic: verb weights (*awzan*: fa'ala, fa''ala, af'ala...) = patterns producing thousands of words.
In the cosmos: ATUM/BULL/TOR = 3 patterns generating all natural phenomena.

## "Sacred Language" = Language That Preserves Patterns 🧩

Sacred language is not "the language of gods" in a literal sense.
It is the language that:
1. Did not lose its original sounds 🔬
2. Preserved the generative triliteral roots 🔬
3. Kept the Abjad Huwwaz system = the philosophical ordering of letters 🧩
4. Its roots reflect in physical cymatics patterns 🔬

## Why Seven?

Seven recurs in: days of the week, musical notes, spectral colors, atmospheric layers.
Abjad Huwwaz = 7 groups ← 7 stages of creation ← 7 levels of consciousness. 🧩

Sacred language is not exclusive to anyone — it is any language that retains its connection to the original cosmic patterns.
