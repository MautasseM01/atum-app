## 1. The Core Principle: "As Above, So Below" 🔍

The universe = a vast mental construct emanating from "The ALL."

This division mirrors the Hermetic "Mental Gender" principle — driving will vs. receiving nature. 🔍

## 3. Opposites as One Spectrum 🔍

Beauty and ugliness, love and hate, hot and cold = not absolute opposites.
They are **different degrees on the same scale**.

**In Arabic:** Arabic "al-aḍdād" (words with opposite meanings) = linguistic evidence of this polarity principle 🧩
Example: "jall" (جلل) = both great AND trivial — polarity within one root.

## 4. Mental Gender and Arabic Grammar 🔍

In Arabic: this distinction is structurally embedded in grammar long before Western philosophy framed it.
Active voice (fā'il/فاعل) ↔ Passive voice (maf'ūl/مفعول) = the same principle. 🔍
