# What Are the Three Roots? ATUM · BULL · TOR

> "Learn the laws, not just the words"

---

## Law One: ATUM 🟢

**Energy:** Unity · Inertia · Beginning

Imagine the nucleus of an atom — fixed at the center, unchanging.
That's ATUM: the thing complete in itself, before any movement.

Words describing something **stable, fundamental, or self-contained** = ATUM:
- Atom, Adam, Anatomy, Autumn, Atlas, Adamant, Atmosphere

---

## Law Two: BULL 🔴

**Energy:** Radiation · Expansion · Outward

Imagine the sun — radiating light and heat in all directions.
That's BULL: the energy that leaves a center and spreads.

Words describing something that **radiates, explodes, or expands** = BULL:
- Bull, Ball, Bloom, Brilliant, Bullet, Bulb, Blue, Baal

---

## Law Three: TOR 🔵

**Energy:** Rotation · Structure · Cycles

Imagine a hurricane — rotating around itself, building its own shape.
That's TOR: the energy that organizes itself into cycles.

Words describing something that **rotates, repeats, or encloses** = TOR:
- Torus, Tornado, Tower, Tour, Territory, Torque, Thor

---

## All Three Together = Everything

| Root | In the Atom | In the Body | In the Cosmos |
|------|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| ATUM | Nucleus | Bone | Center of gravity |
| BULL | Electrons | Blood | Light and heat |
| TOR | Orbitals | Circulation | Planetary orbits |

---

## Why It Matters

Because these three laws are encoded in every language on Earth.
Know ATUM/BULL/TOR — and you'll read words, not memorize them.
Arabic preserves these patterns most clearly — which is why it's the key to understanding all languages.
