## 1. What Is Ibdal? (For New Visitors) 🔬

Ibdal = substituting one letter for another because their articulation points in the mouth or throat are adjacent.
The tongue chooses the easier sound — this is the physics of speech, not randomness.

**Why 75 rules?**
Ancient alphabets (only 22 letters) represented 34 Arabic sounds → 12-sound gap → Orientalists misread words → Al-Qubaysi corrected with 75 rules.
